FilmFunder

Feature Film Crowdfunding

FilmFunder sells profit participation of projects to individual investors, equity funds, and financial institutions who wish to invest in the entertainment industry. The money earned from the sale of profit participation is used to produce the film. FilmFunder fully researches the background of all projects prior to offering them for investment to ensure superior quality and investor protection. Once all of the points have been sold, the funds are released to the project and production can begin. FilmFunder offers a unique investment opportunity built around pre-screened feature films. The online platform acts as a marketplace for investors to purchase profit participation in a film. Each profit participation “point” is sold at a price equal to 1/100th of the total cost to produce the film. Crowdfunding film production has become increasingly popular in the recent months, but professionals are still warming to the concept. The new platform, FilmFunder provides an innovative place for film professionals to conduct transactions with investors to get projects funded. FilmFunder is an advanced tool for film finance professionals. Below you will find a current list of features as well as future features of the FilmFunder Platform.
